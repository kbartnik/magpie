# Martin Fowler

Software engineer and author at Thoughtworks, known for *Refactoring* and *Patterns of Enterprise Application Architecture*. Referenced in this vault for his 2006 concept of [[Semantic Diffusion]] — the process by which precise technical terms lose meaning as they gain popularity.
