# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator, formerly at OpenAI and Tesla. Originator of the LLM Wiki "compile-not-retrieve" paradigm referenced in [[LLM Wiki Pattern]], and the 4-rule CLAUDE.md architecture extended in [[CLAUDE.md Configuration Patterns]]. Known for making deep AI concepts accessible through lectures and writing.
