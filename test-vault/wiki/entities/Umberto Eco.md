# Umberto Eco

Italian semiotician, philosopher, and novelist (1932–2016). In this vault primarily through his 1995 essay *Ur-Fascism*, which provides the 14-feature framework in [[Ur-Fascism]], and his analysis of populist rhetoric in [[Qualitative Populism]]. Also known for *The Name of the Rose* and *Foucault's Pendulum*.
